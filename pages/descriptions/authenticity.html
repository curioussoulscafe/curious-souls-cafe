Private Acts, Public Selves: Authenticity at the Intersection of Identity

(Photo by Felicia Buitenwerf on Unsplash)

A deep-dive into the spaces between who we are, who we show, and how we bridge the gap.

Whether you’re navigating personal reinvention, reconciling private truths with public roles, or reflecting on moments when you’ve hidden (or revealed) parts of yourself, this event offers a space to explore identity, vulnerability, and the courage to live authentically.

Through curated conversational cards and group dialogue, you’ll unpack how we negotiate inner truths with outer expectations--sparking insights about self-acceptance, societal pressures, and the freedom of embracing our full selves.

Come with an open mind, share in enriching discussions, and leave with fresh perspectives on authenticity, identity, and the art of being human.

Inviting all Curious Souls passionate about:

  • 🌍 Embracing open-mindedness.
  • 🤝 Tapping into collective wisdom.
  • 🗣️ Engaging in meaningful conversations.
  • 🌱 Embarking on journeys of self-discovery.
  • 📚 Exploring identity through diverse lenses.

🗓️ Agenda / What You'll Experience

💬 Icebreaker Conversation Cards

We will begin the session with authenticity-themed conversation cards. You'll have the opportunity to pick a card and initiate thought-provoking conversations with fellow attendees. Here's a sneak peek of some of the questions:

  • “When does adapting to others become self-erasure?”
  • “How do private struggles shape our public identities?”
  • “What helps us ‘brave the wilderness’ of our authenticity?”

💬 Discussion on a Spontaneous Question (Optional Round)

Where time allows, our diverse group of friends and strangers will come together to exchange ideas on an intriguing topic chosen spontaneously.

Each participant is encouraged to contribute by sharing a unique question. You’re welcome to bring a question that’s been on your mind, or you can draw inspiration from the icebreaker conversation cards provided. Once all the questions are collected, the group will collaboratively select one to kick off the discussion.

From there, we’ll embark on a respectful, engaging, and meaningful conversation, celebrating the diversity of perspectives and ideas within the group.

💬 Final Thoughts and Feedback

In the final 10 minutes of the event, we’ll take a moment to reflect on the key insights, memorable moments, and takeaways from our discussions. This is a chance to distill the shared wisdom and leave with a deeper understanding of the topics we’ve explored together.

After the event officially concludes, we may choose to extend the session, depending on the group’s interest. Of course, you’re welcome to leave at any time that feels right for you.
