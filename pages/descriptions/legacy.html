üí¨ Icebreaker Conversation Cards

Begin with themed questions to spark intimate and reflective exchanges about the footprints we leave behind. Here are some sample questions:

1. Influence and Impact:

  • "Describe a time someone's simple kindness left a lasting 'imprint' on you. What did it teach you about the legacy of small acts?"
  • "Who is one person (in your life or in history) whose legacy you feel you are building upon? What specifically are you carrying forward from them?"
  • "What is one 'gift' you've received (a skill, a perspective, an opportunity) that you feel responsible for passing on to others?"

2. Deeper Reflection:

  • "Beyond your job title or achievements, what is the one thing you hope people say about your character when you're not in the room?"
  • "If your 'purpose' is your inner compass, what is one small, deliberate step you could take to let it guide your 'outer imprint'?"

Note: This session may involve virtual question cards accessed via a link or QR code. If you do not have a device (phone/tablet) to access these, please ask a facilitator for assistance; we have spare units available.
